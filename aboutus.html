About us


We aspire to provide a digital health Identifier to digitize the health system.

Health Id is an Identification number that will give access to an individual's medical information. It is a convenient resource to make medical information more concise to help EMT's/ER when every second counts. In an emergency when a person is not in a condition to provide doctors with his medical specifications or his emergency contacts or his insurance details in such situations this id will be a lifesaver.

Whom does it benefit

It is especially useful in dire situations
Senior citizens who cannot keep track of their health,Alzeimer patients Doctors for treating new patients,Person with severe food ,drug or insect allergies
Or any health conscious person.

Features

1.All your Healthcare data in one place.
2.Access to crucial medical information in hospitals in exigency
3.Emergency information available without any passcode
4.Your information will remain confidential ,only certified healthcare centers will have access other than yourself.​
5.Insurance details will be accessible easily.
6.Emergency contacts will be informed immediately in case of emergency.
7.User-friendly website
8.Associated with hospitals
